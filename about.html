Our Story

Crafting timeless fashion since 2010

Our Mission

At Elegance, we believe that fashion is more than just clothing—it's a form of self-expression, a confidence booster, and a way to connect with the world around us. Our mission is to create thoughtfully designed pieces that empower individuals to express their unique identity while promoting sustainability and ethical practices in the fashion industry.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through responsible sourcing and production practices.

Quality

We craft each piece with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring longevity and timeless style.

Community

We believe in building meaningful connections and supporting the communities we work with.

Our Beginnings

Elegance was founded in 2010 by fashion enthusiasts Sophie Laurent and Daniel Kim, who shared a vision of creating a brand that celebrates individuality while respecting our planet. What began as a small collection of handcrafted pieces has evolved into a globally recognized brand with a dedicated following.

Our journey started in a small studio in New York, where Sophie and Daniel combined their expertise in design and sustainable practices to create their first capsule collection. Their commitment to quality craftsmanship and ethical production quickly gained attention in the fashion industry.
